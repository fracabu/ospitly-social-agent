### Issue: Slow Follower Growth

**Possible Causes**:
- Not posting consistently
- Content not shareable
- No hashtag strategy
- Not engaging with others
- Account not discoverable

**Solutions**:
- Post minimum 4-5x/week on Instagram, daily on Facebook
- Create highly valuable, shareable content
- Research and use relevant hashtags (10-15 per post)
- Engage with target audience's content (30 min daily)
- Optimize bio, name, and descriptions for keywords
- Run follower growth ad campaigns
- Collaborate with influencers
- Cross-promote on other channels (email, website)

### Issue: Low Website Traffic from Social

**Possible Causes**:
- No clear CTA
- Link not working or too complicated
- Content not compelling enough to click
- Landing page not optimized

**Solutions**:
- Include CTA in every post: "Link in bio"
- Test bio link (click through as user)
- Use link tracking to identify which content drives clicks
- Create dedicated landing pages for social traffic
- Use link shorteners for easier memory
- Make landing pages mobile-optimized and fast
- A/B test different CTAs

- Acknowledge immediately (within 30 minutes)

- Move to DM for resolution
- Do NOT delete unless spam/abusive
- Do NOT argue publicly
- Follow up publicly once resolved
- Learn from feedback
